**Date:** April 13, 2026  
**Context:** Analyzing successful app naming patterns to find the right name

---

## BJJ App Naming Patterns

### Direct/Descriptive Names:

- **BJJ Buddy** - Companion metaphor
- **BJJ Notes** - Straightforward journaling
- **BJJ Training** (App) - Generic but clear
- **Grappling AI** - Tech + activity
- **FlowRoll** - Two concepts combined
- **MatTime** - Training space + time invested
- **Grapplearts** - Activity + art/craft

### What Works:

✅ Combine BJJ concept + app function  
✅ Two-word compounds that tell a story  
✅ Friendly, approachable tone (Buddy, Notes)  
✅ Clear about what they do

## Wellness App Naming Patterns

### State/Concept Names:

- **Headspace** - Mental state you achieve
- **Calm** - Emotional state/outcome
- **Insight Timer** - What you gain + tool
- **Waking Up** - Transformation/awareness
- **Breathwrk** - Core practice
- **Shine** - Positive state/outcome
- **Exhale** - Release/relief action

### What Works:

✅ Names capture the RESULT or STATE you achieve  
✅ Single words that evoke feeling/transformation  
✅ Can be abstract but meaningful  
✅ Not always literal (Headspace isn't about physical space)
