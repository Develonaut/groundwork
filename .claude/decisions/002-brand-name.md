# Brand Name

**Date:** April 13, 2026
**Status:** Accepted
**Context:** Choosing a brand name for the BJJ training journal application

---

## Decision: Groundwork

**Domain:** `groundwork.zone`
**Tagline:** "Build your foundation"

---

## Rationale

1. **Literal BJJ term** — Ground fighting is the core of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. "Groundwork" is instantly recognizable to practitioners
2. **Foundation metaphor** — "Laying the groundwork" = building a foundation for growth. Aligns with the journal's purpose of tracking progress session by session
3. **Swiss Design alignment** — Foundation, structure, grid systems. The name reinforces the design language
4. **Stands alone** — Professional single-word name in the Headspace/Calm tradition. Not generic ("BJJ Notes") or cutesy ("BJJ Buddy")
5. **Unique in the space** — No BJJ apps called "Groundwork"

---

## Domain: groundwork.zone

- "In the zone" — flow state, being present on the mat
- The brand is **Groundwork**, not "Groundwork Zone" — the TLD is just the URL

---

## Voice & Tone

- Calm, focused, intentional
- Professional without being corporate
- Encouraging without being cheesy

**Key phrases:** "Build your foundation" / "Session by session" / "Lay the groundwork"

---

## Alternatives Considered

| Name     | Why Not                                                    |
| -------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Flow     | Too generic, domain conflicts, less BJJ-specific           |
| Frame    | Weaker metaphor, less recognizable as BJJ terminology      |
| Invert   | Too narrow (inversions only), less accessible to beginners |
| Mat Mind | Two words, less clean, alliteration feels forced           |
| Unroll   | Clever but obscure, requires explanation                   |
