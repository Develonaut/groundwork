# Decision 002: Brand Name

**Date:** April 13, 2026  
**Status:** ✅ Decided → **Groundwork**  
**Domain:** `groundwork.zone`  
**Context:** Choosing a brand name for the BJJ training journal application

---

## Final Decision: Groundwork

### Why Groundwork is Perfect

1. **Literal BJJ Term**
   - Ground fighting is the core of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
   - Groundwork = the technical foundation of BJJ
   - Instantly recognizable to practitioners

2. **Powerful Metaphor**
   - Laying the groundwork = building foundation for growth
   - "Do the groundwork" = put in the work, track progress
   - Foundation-building aligns with Swiss Design (structured, grid-based)

3. **Like Headspace/Calm**
   - Single word, professional
   - Evokes a state/outcome (grounded, foundation built)
   - Not cutesy or overly playful

4. **Swiss Design Alignment**
   - Groundwork = foundation, structure, grid systems
   - Visual metaphor: laying down grid lines on whitespace
   - Form follows function (fundamental design principle)

5. **Unique in the Space**
   - No BJJ apps called "Groundwork"
   - Stands apart from "BJJ Buddy", "BJJ Notes", "MatTime"
   - Memorable, distinct identity

## Brand Narrative

> "Every black belt started with groundwork. The techniques, the positioning, the patience—all built on a foundation of consistent practice and reflection. Groundwork is where you lay that foundation. Session by session, insight by insight, you're building something that lasts."

## Domain: groundwork.zone

**URL:** `groundwork.zone`  
**Why .zone:**

- "In the zone" - Flow state, being present
- "Your training zone" - Personal space for growth
- Available and memorable
- Playful TLD, but **not** part of brand identity

**Important:** The brand is **Groundwork**, not "Groundwork Zone". The `.zone` is just the URL.

## Competitive Positioning

**vs BJJ Buddy/BJJ Notes:**

- More refined, less generic
- Swiss Design aesthetic (vs standard app design)
- Foundation/growth narrative (vs tracking stats)

**vs Headspace/Calm (wellness apps):**

- BJJ-specific, not general wellness
- Literal grounding (mat work) + metaphorical grounding (foundation)
- Action-oriented (build) vs passive (relax)

**vs MatTime/FlowRoll:**

- More conceptual depth (foundation metaphor)
- Professional brand identity
- Clear differentiation through design language

---

## Why This is Better Than Other Options

**vs Flow:**

- More BJJ-specific (groundwork is a BJJ term)
- Better metaphor (building vs achieving)
- Stronger narrative (foundation → mastery)
- More distinctive

**vs Frame:**

- Groundwork is more recognizable BJJ terminology
- Foundation metaphor is clearer
- Less likely to conflict with existing products

**vs Invert:**

- Broader concept (not just inversions)
- More accessible to beginners
- Foundation metaphor works for all skill levels
